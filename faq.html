Ticketing

You can purchase a festival pass online through TicketTailor or in person at Red Cat Records or Neptoon Records.

All ticket sales are final.

Yes--Friday and Saturday single-day passes are now available online only.

Please bring one piece of ID with name matching the purchaser of tickets through Ticket Tailor. Festival wristband(s) will be distributed by "will call" at the venue when you check in.

Getting There

The 14, 16, 214, 22, 3, 4, 7 routes all stop with a short walk of the venue--please consult your favourite Maps app for more detailed instructions

Parking at the venue is limited. Please consider carpool, carshare, transit or other arrangements for getting there.

There are a limited number of bike parking spaces available in front of the venue. Please consult a festival volunteer if this is full or you need secure parking for your bike, as we love to encourage cycling to the venue! (Weather permitting.)
